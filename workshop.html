WORKSHOP

Whole or half day workshop sessions are available to schools who would like their pupils to enjoy music in an exciting and accessible way. "Stomp" inspired "Junk Drumming" workshops teach groups of pupils how easy it is to get involved with music, being able to make music with everyday items.
