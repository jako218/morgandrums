SERVICES

Getting new drum equipment can be daunting, even if you have played drums for many years! I offer free advice to my students on what you can buy for your drumming needs, as well as a full set up, tune and dampening service to make sure you get the sound you want from your kit! If you're interested then please send an email to tim@morgandrums.uk and I'll be in touch with more info.
